Bio & Album

XBYRDX is a cracked mirror held up to right-now America — equal parts outlaw crooner and midnight-radio provocateur. Anthems For The End Times rides a cinematic blend of rock swagger, noir twang, and doomsday doo-wop: big hooks, bigger shadows, and lyrics that grin through the smoke.

Featured single: “MAGA Girl” — with companion tracks like “Prizefighter” smashing neon against dust-bowl grit.
